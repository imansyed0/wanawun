Delete your account

You can delete your Wanawun account and associated data at any time.

During private beta, account deletion is handled by email request. An in-app delete option will be available before the public launch.

How to request deletion

  1. From the email address registered to your Wanawun account, send a message to hello@wanawun.com with the subject line "Delete my account".
  2. Confirm in the body that you want your account and data deleted. (We require this confirmation to prevent someone else from deleting your account.)
  3. We'll process the request within 30 days and email you when it's complete.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (display name, email, authentication credentials)
  • Your lesson and clip progress, vocabulary practice records, and game history
  • Your player rating data
  • Any voice recordings you uploaded

What may be retained

We may retain limited records (e.g., aggregate, non-identifying logs, or records required to comply with legal obligations and prevent abuse) for a short period before they too are removed.

Questions

Email hello@wanawun.com if anything is unclear.
